# Swap Radar — First 10 SEO Pages

**Goal:** Own decision-intent search; funnel every page into quiz, calculator, and `/go` clickouts.  
**Voice:** Clear, skeptical-friendly, no hype. Disclose affiliates. Prefer “updated {Month Year}” in H1/intro.  
**Default CTAs:** (1) Take the quiz (2) Open savings calculator (3) Primary platform `/go` links.

---

## Internal link map (use on every page)

```
Pillars ──► Vs pages ──► Platform pages (later) ──► Destinations
   │              │
   └── Quiz ◄─────┴── Calculator
```

Minimum links per page: quiz, `/compare`, one related pillar, one vs or destination.
